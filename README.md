# Routine Tracker

Stay on top of your daily and weekly habits with Routine Tracker — a simple, focused app for building consistent routines.

## What it does

- Add routines with three scheduling options:
  - **Daily** — appears every day
  - **Specific Days** — choose which days of the week (e.g. Mon, Wed, Fri)
  - **Weekly** — complete once any time during the week
- Check off routines as you complete them each day
- Track your **weekly progress** with a live completion bar at the top
- Your data syncs across all your devices — log in on any browser and your routines are there

## How to use it

1. Visit the app at [routine-tracker-liard.vercel.app](https://routine-tracker-liard.vercel.app)
2. Sign in with **Google** or create an account with your email
3. Tap the **+** button to add your first routine
4. Choose a name, pick how often you want to do it, and save
5. Check it off each day as you complete it
